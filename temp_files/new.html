Grozily

Hyperlocal Grocery Delivery for Kashmir

Vendor Terms and Conditions

1. Vendor Onboarding & Verification

Vendor must provide valid business identification documents (e.g., shop license, Aadhaar, GST certificate if applicable).

The Company reserves the right to verify all information and the physical store before approval.

2. Product Listings

Vendor is responsible for uploading accurate product names, pricing, descriptions, and images.

Vendor must update stock regularly and ensure no out-of-stock products are listed.

All listed items must comply with applicable food and consumer safety regulations.

3. Order Handling

Vendors will receive order alerts via the app and must pack and prepare orders within 10–15 minutes.

Repeated delays or cancellations may result in account suspension.

4. Quality & Hygiene

Products must be fresh, sealed properly, and packed hygienically.

Complaints about expired or damaged products may result in refunds, penalties, or delisting.

5. Commission & Payout

The Company will charge a service commission (5%–15%) on each completed order depending on the category and terms.

Payouts will be made weekly, bi-weekly, or instantly after order pickup via UPI or bank transfer.

Earnings and invoices will be available in the vendor dashboard.

6. Pricing and Taxes

Vendor controls product pricing and must include all applicable taxes.

Charging above MRP or breaching platform pricing terms is prohibited.

7. Exclusivity & Branding

Vendor may operate outside the platform but must not use customer data for competing services.

Vendor must not promote competing delivery services through listings or packages.

8. Customer Interaction

Vendor must maintain professional behavior with customers and delivery personnel.

No extra delivery charges may be requested outside the app's billing.

9. Data & Privacy

All customer and transaction data is the property of the Company.

Vendors must not misuse, store, or share customer personal information.

10. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 7-day written notice.

Immediate suspension or termination may occur if terms are violated or customer trust is harmed.

11. Legal Jurisdiction

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Union Territory of Jammu & Kashmir.

Legal disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts located in Srinagar.
