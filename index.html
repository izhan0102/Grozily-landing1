Kashmir's Own Hyperlocal Grocery Delivery Platform

Grozily is a platform built specifically for Kashmir to connect customers with their nearest local kirana shops and general stores. What Makes Grozily Different?

100% Local

Grozily only features verified shops from your area. You are not buying from some unknown supplier far away â€” you're supporting the same local vendors you've been buying from for years, now with added convenience.

No Middlemen

We don't act as a reseller or mark up prices. The shopkeeper receives your order directly and manages the delivery. You get transparent pricing and a more direct, efficient service.

Simple and Lightweight

Our platform is designed to work smoothly, even in areas with slow internet. It uses a clean, minimal interface that loads fast and keeps things easy to use for everyone â€” young or old.

Vendor-Friendly

While most platforms take large commissions, Grozily charges vendors only a 5% platform fee. This allows shopkeepers to maintain fair prices for customers and still earn more. Our goal is to support local business, not take over it.

Deliver and Earn Locally

Grozily is not just for shopping. It's also an opportunity for individuals to earn in their free time. We allow locals to deliver groceries within their own neighborhoods. No bike or vehicle is required â€” you can walk, cycle, or use any available mode of transport.

There is no formal hiring process or contract. If you have a smartphone and are ready to work a few hours a day, you can start delivering and earning with Grozily.

We aim to create job opportunities in small communities, support students, and help anyone looking for extra income.
